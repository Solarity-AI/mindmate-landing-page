How Users Can Delete Their Data

Users can request deletion of all personal data associated with their account at any time. The available methods are:

Users can delete their data by navigating to:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

Users may send a deletion request to: support@solarityai.com

The request must include the email or phone number used in the app.

All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, in accordance with KVKK.

Users may also revoke permissions or delete locally stored data by:

  • Removing the app from their device
  • Resetting app permissions through device settings

Once deletion is complete, all associated personal information, usage data, and stored content are removed from our systems unless required by law to retain.
